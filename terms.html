1. About FightBack NYC

FightBack NYC ("we," "us," or "our") is an informational tool that generates formal appeal letters for app-based delivery workers in New York City who believe their account was wrongfully deactivated.

FightBack NYC is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. No attorney-client relationship is created by using this service. For complex legal matters, we recommend consulting a licensed labor attorney.

2. What Our Service Does

Upon payment, our service generates a personalized appeal letter based on the information you provide. The letter:

  • References applicable NYC laws protecting delivery workers
  • Is formatted for submission to the relevant platform's support or legal team
  • Is delivered digitally, immediately after payment is confirmed

We make no guarantees regarding the outcome of any appeal. Results depend entirely on the platform's review process and the specific circumstances of each case.

⚠️ Important: NYC Local Law protections allow workers to appeal wrongful deactivations and may result in reinstatement and back pay if upheld. The city may also impose fines on platforms found in violation. These outcomes are not automatic and are subject to the platform's and/or DCWP's review.

Non-refundable situations:
