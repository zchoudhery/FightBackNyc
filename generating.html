📬 What to do with your letter

Follow these steps to submit your appeal. The more places you send it, the stronger your case.

1️⃣
Submit in-app first
Open your DoorDash / UberEats / Grubhub app → Go to Help or Support → Find Appeal Deactivation → Copy and paste your letter into the text box → Submit.
2️⃣
Email the platform's legal team
DoorDash: support@doordash.com
Uber Eats: couriersupport@uber.com
Grubhub: driversupport@grubhub.com
Instacart: support@instacart.com

Subject line: "Formal Appeal — Wrongful Deactivation — NYC Local Law 34 of 2026"
Paste your full letter in the email body.
3️⃣
File a complaint with NYC DCWP
If the platform doesn't respond within 5 business days, file a complaint with the NYC Department of Consumer and Worker Protection:

🌐 nyc.gov/dcwp → click File a Complaint
📞 Or call: 311 and say "delivery worker complaint"

The city can investigate and, if violations are found, may impose fines and order remedies.
4️⃣
Keep a record
Screenshot every submission. Save all emails. Write down dates and times. This record is important if the case goes further.
